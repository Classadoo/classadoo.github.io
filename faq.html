FAQ

Who are your teachers?

Our teachers are all passionate educators, who work or have worked at some of the biggest names in tech: Twitter, SpaceX, Uber, Google and more. Check out our teachers page for more info.

What is pricing like?

We generally charge $25 a student. For this we provide one hour long session each week, homeworks (for some courses), and weekly "office hours" when our teachers are available over chat for remote help. However we're happy to set up introductory pricing if you'd like to try out a course. Just contact us!.

What is the structure of a course?

We usually teach one hour long, live, remote class per week for each coures. Then we provide "office hours" - times when our instructors are available to help students 1:1 via chat, during the week Most of the Level II and above courses include projects which students usually work on between classes. If you'd prefer a different structure (more or fewer classes per week, less or more between class work), just get in touch, and we'll design a course that meets your needs!
